The Best Movies According to Shaswati

My top 3 movies of all-time.

12th Fail

It is an inspiring Hindi film based on the real-life story of IPS officer Manoj Kumar Sharma,who overcame extreme poverty and academic failure to succeed in the UPSC exams.

Kabhi khushi kabhi gham

Bollywood family drama that revolves around love, pride, and the bond between parents & children.

Parineeta

It tells the story of Mehul, whose innocent love turns into a gripping tale of mystery.
